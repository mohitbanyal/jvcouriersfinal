Our Company

JV Couriers Ltd, incorporated on 24 February 2023 (Company No. 14688608), is a nationwide courier service provider headquartered at 268 Bath Road, Slough, England. We work with leading logistics partners including DPD, Amazon, Yodel, and Courier Exchange (CX) to provide reliable parcel collection and delivery services across the UK. Our company was established to meet the growing demand for fast, secure, and efficient logistics solutions in the e-commerce and retail sectors. With a dedicated workforce and a growing fleet, we have quickly positioned ourselves as a trusted logistics partner. At JV Couriers Ltd, we are committed to operational excellence, compliance, and customer satisfaction.
